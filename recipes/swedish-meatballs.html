Swedish Meatballs

Description

The iconic IKEA meatballs have always been a delectable treat - but sometimes they are just too far away! Now you can bring this savoury supper to the comfort of your home!

Recipe Credits:

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • 1/4 cup Panko
  • 1 TBSP Parsley chopped
  • 1/4 TSP Allspice
  • 1/4 TSP Nutmeg
  • 1/4 TSP Cloves
  • 1/4 cup onion diced
  • 1 Garlic Clove diced
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 TBSP Butter
  • 1 TBSP Flour
  • 2 cups Beef Broth
  • 1 cup Cream
  • 1 TBSP Worcestshire Sauce
  • 1 TSP Dijon Mustard

Directions

  1. Mix in a mixing bowl ground beef, panko, parsley, allspice, nutmeg, cloves, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and the egg until combined.
  2. Roll into 12 large meatballs or 20 small meatballs (the small ones cook faster and more evenly). Chill in fridge for 15 minutes so meatballs set in shape.
  3. Add a tbsp of butter to a hot pan. Add the meatballs and cook, turning continuously until brown. Trasnfer to a plate and cover with foil
  4. Add flour and butter to pan and whisk continously to form roux. Once desire roux is achieved, slowly pour in beef broth and heavy cream while whisking. Add worcestshire, dijon, and salt & pepper to taste
  5. Add meatballs back to pan and simmer for another minute or two. Best served with mashed potatoes.
