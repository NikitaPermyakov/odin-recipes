Homemade Pizza

Description

One of the world's most versatile and most delicious foods imaginable, it is very simple to make pizza dough at home. The toppings can be literally anything you want!

Recipe Credit

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 packet Instant Yeast
  • 1 1/2 TSP Sugar
  • 3/4 TSP Salt
  • [OPTIONAL] 1/4 TSP Garlic Powder
  • 2 TBSP Olive Oil
  • 3/4 cup Water

Directions

  1. Combine 1 cup flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and optional garlic powder in a large bowl
  2. Add olive oil and water and use wooden spoon to stir very well
  3. Gradually add another cup of flour, and any additional flour as needed, stirring until dough forms into slightly sticky but cohesive ball
  4. Drizzle a seperate large bowl with olive oil, brushing it over the sides
  5. Transfer your dough to the oiled bowl, making sure it is covered
  6. Preheat oven to 425F while allowing dough to rise until doubled in size - about 30 minutes.
  7. Deflate dough and transfer to lightly floured work surface and knead for 3 - 5 minutes.
  8. Work dough into a circle, pinch edge over to form a crust.
  9. Poke holes in the pizza to prevent it from bubbling in the oven
  10. Add desired toppings and back for 13 - 15 minutes.
