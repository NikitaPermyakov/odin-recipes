Meat Buns

Description

Combining a delicious, savoury, and meaty filling with a soft and delectable brioche bread container - these meat buns are a blast to make and to eat!

Recipe credits:

Ingredients

Filling
  • 1 LB Beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp Bouillon Powder
  • 2 Carrots
  • 2 green onions
  • Salt, Pepper, Red Chili Flakes to taste
  • 1/2 cup water + 2 TBSP slurry
Buns
  • 2 1/2 cups Flour
  • 2 TBSP Butter
  • 1/2 TBSP Yeast
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 3 TBSP Sugar
  • 1/2 TSP Salt

Directions

Buns
  1. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm milk and leave to proof.
  2. Mix flour, salt and sugar in a large bowl. Make well at the center and add proofed yeast, egg, melted butter and mix until soft dough is formed.
  3. Transfer the dough to lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic - about 7 minutes
  4. Place dough inside large oiled bowl, and leave to rise in warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size. Work on filling in the meantime
  5. Punch down dough to remove trapped air and transfer to lightly floured work surface.
  6. Divide dough into 6 equal balls
  7. Roll each ball of dough into a flat disc, scoop filling into the center and then gether dough at the top. Pinch the top a couple of times to secure the dough
  8. Place seam side down on parchment-lined baking sheet. Cover and let rise until doubled - about 30 minutes
  9. Brush with egg wash and back at 350 degrees for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
Filling
  1. Sautee the diced onions in pan while dough is rising
  2. Add beef and break it up
  3. Add the thume, red chili flakes, grated carrots, green onions, bouillon powder, and salt & pepper to taste
  4. Cook until ground beef is cooked
  5. Add the slurry and leave to simmer for a few mor minutes. Remove from heat to cool.
